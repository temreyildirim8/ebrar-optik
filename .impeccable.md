# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Local residents of Kırıkkale, Turkey seeking optical services. They visit the site to:
- Find contact information and location
- Browse available frame collections
- Learn about services (prescription glasses, sunglasses, lenses)
- Verify the business is trustworthy before visiting in person

**Context:** Mobile-first users often checking on-the-go, looking for quick confirmation that Ebrar Optik is a reliable local optician. Users range from young adults to elderly, requiring clear typography and accessible design.

### Brand Personality
**Three words:** Trusted, Professional, Established

- **Voice:** Warm but authoritative, healthcare-focused yet approachable
- **Tone:** Confident without being clinical, helpful without being pushy
- **Personality:** The neighborhood optician you trust with your family's eye care - experienced, caring, and reliable

### Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone:** Clean, professional, slightly warm

- **Current palette:** Stone neutrals (warm grays) with emerald green accents
- **Typography:** Inter font - modern, highly legible, professional
- **Effects:** Subtle glassmorphism, smooth scroll animations, no excessive decoration
- **Theme:** Light mode primary, dark mode supported

**Design approach:**
- Minimal but not sterile
- Professional without being corporate
- Warm without being playful
- Turkish-first with proper localization

### Design Principles

1. **Trust First** - Every element should reinforce credibility. Avoid trendy effects that could undermine professionalism. Use real photos, clear information hierarchy, and established UI patterns.

2. **Accessibility for All Ages** - Local customers span all demographics. Prioritize legibility (sufficient contrast, large touch targets), clear navigation, and reduced motion options.

3. **Subtle, Not Flashy** - Animations should guide attention, not distract. The product (eyewear, services) is the hero, not the interface effects.

4. **Mobile-First Local** - Most visitors check on phones before visiting. Ensure critical info (location, hours, contact) is immediately visible on mobile.

5. **Cultural Authenticity** - Turkish language throughout, local business hours format, WhatsApp as primary contact channel, familiar patterns for Turkish users.

## Technical Notes

- **Framework:** Next.js 15+ (App Router, static export)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS 4+ with shadcn/ui components
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for scroll reveals
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Icons:** Lucide React + custom SVGs
- **Content Protection:** Basic right-click/selection prevention enabled
